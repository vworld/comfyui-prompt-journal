## ShotGenerationBrowser

Display ASCII artefacts

◀  No Shot Selected  ▶  │  History ▲

◀  7 / 7 Generations  ▶  │  History ▲



On clicking History button a popover opens above the button showing details about all generations related to the shot

where the format is

#<attempt_num> <output_file_name> (<added_on>)

- attempt_num being the index of the gen after sorting by added_on, asc



Icons being

▶ = currently loaded generation

○ = pending review

(blank) = reviewed generation
